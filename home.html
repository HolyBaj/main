Safety Tips

"Staying safe is not an accident; it's a conscious choice fueled by awareness and precaution."

  • Train users on gas stove monitoring to emphasize the importance of promptly reporting any issues.

  • Place gas leak detectors near the stove and regularly test them to promptly detect any leaks.

  • Display the emergency contact number to ensure everyone knows who to call in case of an emergency.
